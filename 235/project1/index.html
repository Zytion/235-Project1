Overview

Meat Clicker

Meat Clicker is a game where you start off with a single resource that you can click to gather to build your village into the capital of the world. Once the player has gathered enough meat to gain the attention of a worker, they can then allocate that worker to a job that will idly gain resources. You can also use certain resources to upgrade the worker's speed and/or worker's output.

Stuff

Resources

The resources tab that you can gather are: meat (for workers), wood (for structures/upgrades), stone (for structures/upgrades), and ore (upgrades). You will be able to hover over the resources to see how many resources you are getting per tick.
