Overview

Meat Clicker

Meat Clicker is a game where you start off with a single resource that you can click to gather to build your village into the capital of the world. Once the player has gathered enough meat to gain the attention of a worker, they can then allocate that worker to a job that will idly gain resources. You can also use certain resources to upgrade the worker's speed and/or worker's output.

Explanation

Resources

The resources tab that you can gather are: meat (for workers), wood (for structures/upgrades), stone (for structures/upgrades), and ore (upgrades). You will be able to hover over the resources to see how many resources you are getting per tick.

Population

The population tab contains all the jobs that your workers can take on. There is the hunter (gathers meat), miner (gathers stone/ore), and lumberjack (gathers wood). You will be able to hover over the jobs to see how much they will add per tick.

Build

The build tab has all the buildings that you can build. There is the house (allows for more workers to join), mineshaft (increases the number of miners you can have), lumberyard (increases the number of lumberjacks), and lodge (a different form of the house that can hold more people but will generally be more expensive).

Upgrades

The upgrades tab has all the upgrades you can purchase with your resources. There is the hunter (speeds up worker's production), recruiter (recruits workers faster), townmaster (increases housing), pickaxe (increases worker's production), hatchet (increases worker's production), spears (increases worker's production).
